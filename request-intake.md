# Request Intake

## Request Types
- New capability request
- Change to existing contract
- Exception request
- Incident follow-up contract update

## Required Inputs
- Business objective
- Target launch date
- Affected service(s)
- Consumer team and owner
- Required fields / schema examples
- Risk or compliance constraints

## SLA Targets
- Acknowledge: 1 business day
- Triage decision: 3 business days
- Contract proposal: 5 business days (for standard scope)
