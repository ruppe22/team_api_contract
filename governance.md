# Governance

## Versioning
- **Major**: Breaking contract changes.
- **Minor**: Backward-compatible fields/capabilities.
- **Patch**: Clarifications, typo fixes, non-functional updates.

## Deprecation
- Minimum deprecation window for breaking changes: 90 days.
- Changelog entry and migration guidance required.
- Consumer impact must be documented before approval.

## Approval Flow
1. Draft change proposal.
2. Technical owner review.
3. Product owner approval.
4. Publish to changelog and notify consumers.
