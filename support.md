# Support Model

## Channels
- Intake queue: see `request-intake.md`
- Office support: async channel during business hours
- Incident bridge: Sev 1 / Sev 2 only

## Response Expectations
- Sev 1: immediate triage
- Sev 2: triage within 1 hour
- Standard requests: see SLA targets in `request-intake.md`

## Escalation
Escalate only under the criteria listed in the README escalation section.
